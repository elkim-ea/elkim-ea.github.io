- Hierarchical : Arranged in levels or layers — from top to bottom, like a tree.

- Domain Registrar : A company where you can buy and register a domain name (like mywebsite.com).

- Mandatory : Required. You must do it. No choice.

- Functionality : What something can do — its features or abilities.

- Super : In AWS, often used in programming (like super() in code) meaning "parent" or "higher".

- Throttles : Slows down how often something can happen — limits speed or rate.

- Integrated : Combined or connected together smoothly.

- Metrics : Numbers/data that help you measure performance or behavior.

- Threshold : A limit — when something passes this value, action is taken.

- Interval : A set time gap between two events.

- Precise : Very exact, accurate.

- Restrict : Limit what someone can do or access.

- Geolocation : Detects user's physical location using IP.

- Geoproximity : Routing users based on location and distance, with optional bias.

- Bias : A setting to adjust traffic routing toward or away from a region.

- Corresponding : Matching or related to something else.

- Registrar : A company where you register your domain name.
