- progression : Step-by-step movement forward or upward.

- vertically : Up and down direction (like a ladder ⬆️⬇️).

- horizontally : Side to side (like adding more computers ➡️⬅️).

- disasters : Serious failure or problem, like system crash or fire.

- Affinity : A preference or connection/relationship between resources.

- stack : A group of resources deployed together in AWS (like in CloudFormation).

- beforehand : Something done early, in advance.

- format : The structure or style of something (data, file, etc.).

- centric : "focused on" something.

- component : A part or piece of a larger system.

- provision : To set up or allocate resources.

- underlying : The base layer or foundation something is built on.

- iteration : One repeat/loop of a process — often used in development.
